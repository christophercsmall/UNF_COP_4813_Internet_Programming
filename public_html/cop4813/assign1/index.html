Personal Details

Past

About a year or so after I graduated High School, I enlisted in the US Navy as an Operations Specialist and was stationed aboard the USS Cowpens (CG-63) in Yokosuka, Japan. Shortly after I completed my four year enlistment, I began my pursuit of a career in Information Technology/Systems at ITT Technical Institute, earned a two year degree, then moved on to Florida State College at Jacksonville, and finally transfered to The University of North Florida.

Present

I am years old, I live and work in Jacksonville, Florida, and am currently an undergraduate student in my senior year at The University of North Florida. I work for SMG as an IT technician servicing Jacksonville's major venues including Everbank Field, The Jacksonville Veteran's Memorial Arena, and Prime Osborn Convention Center. I enjoy both web and software development in my spare time, so I think I can consider that to be the closest thing I can call a hobby, as I spend a lot of my free time working on personal programming projects and reading about new development technologies.


Future

I intend to graduate near the end of 2016. Though I enjoy my current work in IT, after graduation I feel I should eventually take advantage of my degree in order to find employment that falls more squarely in line with everything I have studied and practiced in Information Systems. I plan to live and work in Jacksonville while it continues to grow unless greater opportunites become present elsewhere.
